Croissants are a delight whether served for breakfast or brunch, but sadly, that is not something a vegan-from-birth could ever know because croissants are not vegan! A description of a good croissant is more or less legally required to include the words buttery and flaky and, as anyone who has ever watched The Great British Bake Off will know, it is the butter that is vital to the lamination that creates the flaky texture. It is also pretty vital to the buttery-ness! Whilst even non-bakers will probably know that a croissant contains butter (and lots of it!), perhaps not everyone would be aware that the initial dough that is then layered with the butter also includes milk.
